"Debate Topics for Filipinos"

Blog posted on 6-11-2022

In trying to find good topics to discuss here on this here blog of mine, I realized there’s not too many resources with Filipino-centered debate topics or essay topics.

So let’s try to compose a list of debate topics that could apply in the Philippines:

Aesthetics

- This House believes beauty pageants help foster healthy standards of beauty for Filipinos

Technology

- This House believes artificial intelligence technology is going to put many people, across the globe, out of work

- This House believes Filipino children should be exposed to mobile technology as soon as possible

- This House believes vaping should be banned

- This House believes Mobile Legends, and similar MOBAs, is a healthy social development tool for the youth

Health and Medicine

- This House believes reproductive health programs need to be implemented in the Philippines

- This House believes marijuana should be legalized

- This House believes abortion should not be legalized

- This House believes euthanasia, for persons with a terminal illness, should be legalized in the Philippines

Economy and Industry

- This House believes foreign investors should be able to own 100% of their businesses in the Philippines

- This House believes the Philippines fosters an investment friendly climate

- This House believes the Philippines should shoulder all the domestic costs in the fight against climate change

- This House believes global warming is a real phenomenon occurring across the globe wrecking havoc on regional weather

- This House believes the Philippines needs to open new nuclear power plants for more energy production

- This House believes the Philippines should utilize the Bataan Nuclear Power Plant again

- This House believes the Overseas Filipino Worker (OFW) phenomenon needs to continue in order to help keep the Philippine economy strong

- This House supports the Philippines in the continuing production of large quantities of Filipino nurses

- This House believes that the “Filipino First” idea, and its succeeding provisions in law, is effective at developing the Philippine economy

- This House believes the Comprehensive Agriculture Reform Law (CARL) has been effective at developing productivity in the Philippine agricultural sector

- This House believes minimum wages across all Philippine regions should be increased to more livable wages

- This House believes money should be abolished

Politics

- This House believes a SOGIE Bill needs to be implemented in law

- This House believes the Presidential System, as a whole, in the Philippines is working effectively for the citizens of the Philippines

- This House believes federalism will help to empower the Philippine regions

- This House believes parliamentary government will help bring out better party politics in the Philippines

- This House believes the party-list system, for the House of Representatives, is working in the Philippines

- This House believes we should develop towards a two-party system for national governance

- This House believes all political dynasties in the Philippines should be banned

- This House believes the Sangguniang Kabataan should be abolished

- This House believes the BARMM and Cordilleras regions must inspire the other regions to become autonomous

- This House believes there should not be a divorce law

- This House believes the Philippines national language provision in the constitution hinders the growth of many other Philippine languages

- This House believes there is a strong enforcement of the “separation of church and state” principle in the Philippine political arena

- This House supports same-sex marriage in the Philippines

- This House believes the Philippines should not be aggressive over its possession of territory in the West Philippine Sea

- This House believes that electing out corrupt officials in the Philippines is more important than changing how officials get elected to begin with

Education

- This House believes dress codes in universities are too strict

- This House believes the free tuition law is not helping the average less fortunate student in the long run

- This House believes sex education should be required in high school

- This House believes that Filipino college/university debates should be held only in English or Filipino

- This House believes the K-12 Program has been and currently is an effective program for education

- This House believes the subject of Mother Tongue Language should be taken out of the Philippine curriculum

- This House believes the subject of Philippine History should be placed back into the Philippine curriculum

- This House believes that historical distortion about the Martial Law years is occurring in the Philippines

- This House believes social media should not be used as an assignment tool with the use of likes and shares in order to garner grades

- This House believes that homework should be abolished

Fun

- This House believes Superman and Clark Kent are not the same person

- This House believes Iron Man, from Marvel Comics, is the greatest superhero of all time

- This House believes ‘ghosting’ (by a significant other) should be punishable by law

Philosophical

- This House believes that money is the root of all evil

- This House believes that there is an afterlife

- This House believes that maximizing one’s own happiness is the true meaning of life

- This House believes that maximizing one’s own pleasures is the true meaning of life

- This House believes in the saying, “The unexamined life is not worth living”

- This House believes that present-day human beings descended from a line of ancient primates through a process called evolution
